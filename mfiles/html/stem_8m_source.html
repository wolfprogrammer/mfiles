00018 ## @deftypefn {Function File} {@var{h} =} stem (@var{x}, @var{y}, @var{linespec})
00019 ## Plot a stem graph and return the handles of the line and marker
00020 ## objects used to draw the stems.  The default color is @code{"r"}
00021 ## (red). The default line style is @code{"-"} and the default marker is
00022 ## @code{"o"}.
00023 ##
00024 ## For example,
00025 ## @example
00026 ## x = 1:10;
00027 ## stem (x);
00028 ## @end example
00029 ## @noindent
00030 ## plots 10 stems with heights from 1 to 10;
00031 ##
00032 ## @example
00033 ## x = 1:10;
00034 ## y = ones (1, length (x))*2.*x;
00035 ## stem (x, y);
00036 ## @end example
00037 ## @noindent
00038 ## plots 10 stems with heights from 2 to 20;
00039 ## 
00040 ## @example
00041 ## x = 1:10;
00042 ## y = ones (size (x))*2.*x;
00043 ## h = stem (x, y, "b");
00044 ## @end example
00045 ## @noindent
00046 ## plots 10 bars with heights from 2 to 20
00047 ## (the color is blue, and @var{h} is a 2-by-10 array of handles in
00048 ## which the first row holds the line handles and the 
00049 ## the second row holds the marker handles);
00050 ##
00051 ## @example
00052 ## x = 1:10;
00053 ## y = ones (size (x))*2.*x;
