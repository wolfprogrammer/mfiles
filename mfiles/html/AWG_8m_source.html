00042 Circ-mils = Cross sectional Area in Circular Mils. ( circular mils = diameter in mils squared )
00043 Ohms/Kft = Ohms Per 1,000 ft.

00045 Ft/Lb = Feet Per Pound
00046 Ohms/Lb = Ohms Per Pound
00047 Lb/Kft = Pounds Per 1,000 feet
00048 *AMPS = Conservative Amp Rating based on 750 circulare mils per Amp
00049 MaxAmps = Maximum allowable current based on 500 circular mils per Amp. Do NOT exceed this rating.
